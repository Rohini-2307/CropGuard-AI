FAQ

How disease detection works?

The crop simulates image-based AI prediction and displays confidence, symptoms, treatment, prevention, and recovery guidance.

How accurate is AI?

This crop is presentation-ready logic. A production version should connect a trained crop disease model and validate it with field images.

Which crops are supported?

The crop supports common crop workflows for tomato, rice, chilli, cotton, maize, and generic crops.

How weather recommendations work?

Weather risk uses temperature, humidity, wind, and rain probability to generate irrigation and disease-risk alerts.

How profit estimation works?

It combines land area, estimated yield, current crop market price, and farmer input costs.
